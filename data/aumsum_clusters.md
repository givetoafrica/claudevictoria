## Key reads
- Fantasy planets (Cluster 1) average 2-3x the legacy What-If clusters and hold
  the #1 and #2 spots; this is the format the algorithm currently rewards on
  this channel.
- Space-scale premises beat human-scale premises across the board:
  clusters 1-4 (space) average ~450K+, clusters 5-7 (human/absurd) ~230K.
- Short 2-6 minute disaster stories (Magnetic Storm 726K at 5:43, Sun's Dark
  Transformation 615K at 2:51) earn near-top views at a fraction of runtime —
  best views-per-production-minute on the channel.
- The long "+ more videos" compilations (30-60 min) still pull mid-tier views
  from library/autoplay traffic but none crack the top 3 except the digestive
  compilation.
