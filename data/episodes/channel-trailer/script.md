# Channel Trailer — "It's All Real" (~40 seconds)

Channel: Better Than Fiction · Locked style (data/script-style-guide.md)
Purpose: "For new visitors" trailer. Convince a stranger in 10 seconds that
everything on the channel is real, introduce the cast, ask for the subscribe.
Cast: Narrator (percy voice) · Explorer (sparky voice) · Rex (prof voice)

---

**NARRATOR:** See this planet? It looks calm. It looks blue. It looks... nice.

**REX:** It is NOT nice. It rains GLASS there. SIDEWAYS.

**NARRATOR:** And that planet is real.

*(quick montage — one second each)*

**NARRATOR:** So is the planet as fluffy as cotton candy. And the storm
bigger than the whole Earth — that never, ever stops. And the star that
could go BOOM... any day now.

**EXPLORER:** Wait wait wait. ALL of that is REAL?!

**NARRATOR:** All of it. Nobody made it up. Scientists found every single
one — hiding in the light of the stars.

**REX:** I checked the math. Twice. Unfortunately... it's true.

**NARRATOR:** I'm the Narrator. That's our explorer. The grumpy one is Rex.

**REX:** I am not grumpy. I am *accurate*.

**NARRATOR:** Every week, we visit the strangest TRUE places in the
universe. Simple words. Silly jokes. Zero made-up stuff.

**EXPLORER:** So if it sounds impossible...

**NARRATOR:** ...it's probably on this channel. Subscribe — because the
real universe is better than fiction.

**REX:** Sideways. Glass. I still can't believe my eye.

---

## Visual plan
1. (0–5s) Calm blue Glass Storm Planet → whip-pan INTO the sideways glass
   storm on Rex's line.
2. (5–12s) One-second montage beats: pink fluffy planet · Earth-sized storm
   swirl · ominous pulsing red star. Small orange "REAL" tag stamped on each.
3. (12–20s) Starlight through a planet's rim splitting into a spectrum
   (the detective shot).
4. (20–28s) Cast lineup on the dark ridge from the banner; Rex slow-blinks
   on "accurate."
5. (28–40s) Logo end card: navy, wordmark, orange subscribe pulse, Rex's
   eye glint.

## Notes
- ~105 words narration ≈ 40s with beats.
- Montage previews Ep1 (glass rain), Ep2 (cotton candy), and two future
  episodes (the endless storm, the ready-to-blow star) — a season promise.
- Until produced, Episode 1's cold open can serve as the interim trailer.
