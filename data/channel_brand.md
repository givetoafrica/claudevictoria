Welcome to Better Than Fiction — where every amazing thing is REAL.

strangest true places in the universe. Simple words, good jokes, and 100%
real science — no jargon, no fiction, no exceptions.

New episode every week. Subscribe, and remember: the real universe is
better than fiction.

## Audience setting
Channel-level: "No, it's not made for kids" — review per video.
Positioning rule: the channel targets a GENERAL audience. The simple
all-ages script style is a clarity choice, not kid-targeting. Therefore:
- Never use "kids", "children", "for kids", "family friendly" in any
  title, description, tag, keyword, playlist name, or thumbnail.
- No nursery-styled packaging; art stays cinematic/painterly.
- Marketing voice speaks to curious people of every age, never to
  children specifically.
